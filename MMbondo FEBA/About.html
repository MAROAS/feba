Our Mission

Our mission is to unite the Babondo community in Australia and across the diaspora, working together to seek lasting peace in Eastern DRC while providing vital assistance to those displaced by war. We are committed to fostering dialogue, promoting solidarity, and supporting sustainable development initiatives that empower communities affected by conflict.

Through advocacy, education, and direct aid, we strive to create a future where all Congolese can live in peace, dignity, and prosperity.

Join us in building peace and supporting those in need.
