Learn More About FEBA

Discover how FEBA is making a difference in the fight against hunger in the DRC. We provide food and essential support to communities displaced by conflict, working closely with local partners to ensure aid reaches those most in need. Beyond immediate relief, our programs strengthen resilience by supporting local farmers and promoting sustainable solutions, helping families rebuild their livelihoods in the aftermath of war.

How You Can Help

There are many ways to get involved and support our cause:

  • Donate: Your financial support can help us provide meals, resources, and support to those in need.
  • Volunteer: Join our team of volunteers to help with food distribution, fundraising events, and community outreach.
  • Spread the Word: Share our mission and work on social media to raise awareness about hunger in the DRC.
  • Partner with Us: If you represent an organization or business, consider partnering with us to amplify our impact.

Together, we can make a difference in the lives of those affected by hunger in the DRC. Join us in our mission to create a world where everyone has access to nutritious food.
